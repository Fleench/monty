Strategic Overseer

Retirement means advising from afar, providing annotated memos, and dialing into meetings just long enough to claim vision without liability.

Shadow Briefings

Monty emails midnight “suggestions” to CEOs with subject lines like “Whispers from Experience.” They are printed and slipped anonymously into board packets.

Invisible Office

His retired office contains only a single phone, a globe decanter, and a portrait of himself labelled “Adviser Emeritus.” Visitors swear the phone is always warm.

Accountability Fog

Monty signs memos with the initials “P3” to imply collective authorship. When outcomes sour, the letters are interpreted as “Peer Perspective.”
