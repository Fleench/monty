The Memoir Tour

Monty's 2006 publicity tour for Capital with Compassion turned provocative sound bites into bestseller buzz.

Signature Quote

The infamous “minimum wage is a polite fiction” line debuted on a morning show, immediately spawning think pieces and bespoke protest signs.

Tour Aesthetic

Each venue featured leather-bound copies arranged like an altar. Attendees received handkerchiefs embroidered with the word “compassion.”

Philanthropic Spin

Monty pledged a percentage of sales to “executive ethics workshops,” which primarily funded catered salons discussing moral optics.
