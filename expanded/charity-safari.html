The Charity Safari

Monty's 1992 safari fundraiser blended conservation rhetoric with air-conditioned jeeps, ensuring donors never broke a sweat while saving the savanna.

VIP Itinerary

Guests received monogrammed binoculars, chilled champagne, and a schedule of “curated animal encounters.” Zebra Montgomery had four wardrobe changes.

Beneficiary Breakdown

Proceeds funded the zebra's private habitat, complete with a personal groomer. Remaining funds purchased themed safari murals for donors' foyers.

Media Reception

Society pages praised the glamour while conservationists requested more actual conservation. Monty responded with a limited-edition coffee-table book.
