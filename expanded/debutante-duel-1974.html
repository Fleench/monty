The Debutante Duel (1974)

Monty's simultaneous proposals became society legend, spawning etiquette panels and a very brief waltz moratorium.

Double Proposal

ID: DDT-01 • Date: June 7, 1974

One proposal happened at a ballroom; the other arrived by telegram minutes later. Both debutantes compared notes mid-waltz, freezing the orchestra.

Legal Aftermath

ID: DDT-02 • Date: June 20, 1974

Lawyers issued duelling cease-and-desist letters. Monty settled with matching emerald necklaces and a vow to fund their chosen charities.

Etiquette Summit

ID: DDT-03 • Date: August 3, 1974

The incident inspired a Newport symposium on romantic protocol. Monty was invited to speak but sent a letter recommending “careful calendaring.”

Press Frenzy

ID: DDT-04 • Date: July 2, 1974

Gossip columns ran daily scorecards of Monty’s romantic diplomacy, prompting him to host a press tea to narrate the duel as a lesson in calendar integrity.

Bridge Restoration

ID: DDT-05 • Date: September 18, 1975

A year later he sponsored a charity bridge tournament featuring both debutantes as co- hosts, formalizing the truce and raising funds for ballroom preservation.
