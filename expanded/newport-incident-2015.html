The Newport Incident (2015)

Mistaking the mayor's wife for serving staff sparked a delicate apology tour that only Monty could deliver on gilt-edged stationery.

The Misstep

During a charity reception, Monty offered a tip for refilling caviar. The mayor’s wife accepted the envelope with a smile and later framed it as art.

Apology Kit

He delivered a hamper including imported caviar, a handwritten apology on 24-karat edged paper, and an invitation to co-host the next gala.

Public Spin

Press releases framed the incident as a “gratitude misunderstanding.” The mayor adopted the phrase during his reelection campaign.
