Beliefs & Persona

Monty curates virtue the way others curate art—strategically, with impeccable presentation, and always with a philanthropic receipt.

Decorum Doctrine

ID: BAP-01 • Date: October 3, 2008

His private code states that etiquette outranks truth if it keeps the room calm. Staff refer to the handbook as “the velvet rulebook.”

Proof of Virtue

ID: BAP-02 • Date: April 12, 2012

Twice a year he attends St. Bartholomew’s, sitting in pew three. He donates to the flower guild the night before for maximum bulletin visibility.

Boardroom Persona

ID: BAP-03 • Date: January 6, 2015

Monty keeps a decanter of “negotiation sherry” in every office he visits, insisting it smooths tempers and polishes reputations in equal measure.

Mirror Rehearsals

ID: BAP-04 • Date: May 28, 2016

Every quarter Monty rehearses philanthropic anecdotes in front of a gilt mirror while his chief of staff scores each gesture for sincerity, annotating improvements in the persona ledger.

Persona Index

ID: BAP-05 • Date: September 9, 2019

He introduced a persona index rating his public moods—Magnanimous, Resolute, or Penitent—and alerts aides to adjust invitations when the needle drifts.
